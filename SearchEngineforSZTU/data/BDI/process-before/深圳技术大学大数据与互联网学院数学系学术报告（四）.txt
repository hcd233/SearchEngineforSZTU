深圳技术大学大数据与互联网学院数学系学术报告（四） 报告标题：Sullivan's dictionary, conformal dynamics, and Assouad type dimensions 报 告 人：Prof. Jonathan Fraser 报告时间：2022年11月10日（星期四），18：00―19：00 报告地点：（Zoom）会议号：841 7271 6246 ；密码：standrews 邀 请 人：陈海鹏 博士 摘 要： The Sullivan dictionary provides a conceptual framework to compare the actions of Kleinian groups and the dynamics of rational maps. Both of these settings generate beautiful fractal sets and the dictionary provides a particularly strong correspondence concerning the dimension theory of these sets. For example, restricting to the geometrically finite setting, in both cases there is a 'critical exponent' which returns the Hausdorff, box, and packing dimensions of the associated fractal set. We show that by expanding the family of dimensions considered to include the 'Assouad type dimensions' a richer dimension theory emerges. This allows us to draw new comparisons, and also provide novel discrepancies, between the Kleinian and rational map settings.
